How to use Reader

  • Find the icon — Reader lives in your browser toolbar. If you don't see the ◐ icon, click the puzzle-piece (Extensions) button and pin Reader so it's always one click away.
  • Read a page — on any article, click the Reader toolbar icon to open the popup, then press Read this page. The clean version opens here and is saved to your library.
  • Import files — Import file in the sidebar reads PDF and EPUB files from your computer as articles.
  • Library — saved articles sit in the sidebar on the left. Click one to open it, 📌 to pin it to the top, ✕ to delete, Clear all to wipe everything.
  • Select — Select turns the list into checkboxes; click anywhere on an item to choose it, then mark read, export, or delete several at once.
  • Search & filter — the box at the top filters by title, synopsis, or URL. The ▾ button beside it filters by Unread or Completed (press Save to apply).
  • Status — each item shows Unread (red) or Read (green) once you reach the end. Opening a fresh tab picks up wherever you last left off.
  • Settings — the ⚙ button changes theme, the theme-change animation, reading font, text size, line height, letter spacing, width, library sort, and sidebar mode. Reset settings at the bottom restores the defaults.
  • Sidebar modes — Pinned (always open), Hover / Reveal (slide in from the left edge), or Hidden (a small handle on the left brings it back). The ‹ in the header also hides it.
  • Highlight & notes — select any text, then pick a colour. Choose Note (or click an existing highlight) to attach a note; the ✎ dock button reviews them all.
  • Focus mode — ⚙ → Reading aids (or f) dims everything but a window of lines around what you're reading; size it with Focus window.
  • Read aloud — ⚙ → Read article aloud (or r) speaks from wherever you are. While it plays, click any paragraph to jump there.
  • Download & original — the ⬇ dock button saves the article (with highlights) as Markdown; the ↗ button beside the sidebar opens the original source.
  • Back to top — the ↑ beside the READER title in the sidebar (and g g) jumps to the top; the bar across the very top shows your progress.
  • Shortcuts & tips — press ? for every keyboard shortcut, and hover any icon button to see what it does.
